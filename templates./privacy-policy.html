Privacy Policy for OrionAC - SalesOS

Effective Date: 25/09/2025

OrionAC (“we,” “our,” or “the Company”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and secure information when you connect your email or other accounts to our product, SalesOS, for sales automation purposes.

1. Data We Collect and How We Use It

When you use SalesOS, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Email addresses, account identifiers, and authentication tokens.
  • Email Content & Metadata: Subject lines, sender/recipient information, timestamps, and other relevant content needed for automation.
  • Contacts & Calendars: Only if required for sending or scheduling communications.
  • Device & Usage Data: IP addresses, device type, location (if necessary), and platform usage metrics.

Purpose:

We use this data to:

  • Automate sales emails, follow-ups, and other communications.
  • Provide analytics and performance insights.
  • Prevent fraud, spam, and abuse.
  • Improve and maintain SalesOS.

Recommendation:

For safety and privacy, we strongly advise creating a dedicated account for SalesOS. Avoid using personal or sensitive email accounts. This ensures your main email remains private and reduces risk.

2. User Consent & Control

  • You must provide explicit consent before SalesOS can access any connected account.
  • Consent is granular—you can choose which actions SalesOS is allowed to perform (e.g., read inbox, send emails, access contacts).
  • You can revoke or modify permissions at any time via your account settings.

3. Sharing Your Data

  • We do not sell your data.
  • Data may be shared with trusted third-party services (e.g., analytics, cloud storage) only to provide SalesOS functionality.
  • We ensure safeguards like encryption, limited access, and contracts to protect your data.

4. Security of Your Data

  • We use encryption in transit and at rest.
  • Strict access controls and monitoring protect your data.
  • In case of a breach, we will notify you promptly.
  • We comply with security standards required by connected services (e.g., Gmail, Outlook).

5. Automated Actions & AI

  • SalesOS automates email sending, scheduling, and follow-ups.
  • Some features use AI to optimize messaging and timing.
  • You can opt out of automation for specific tasks at any time.
  • Automated actions comply with service provider rules to prevent spam or abuse.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

  • We retain data only as long as needed for SalesOS functionality.
  • When you delete your account or disconnect a service, your data will be removed upon request.
  • You can request deletion of your data at any time through your account settings or by contacting support.

7. Regulatory Compliance

  • OrionAC and SalesOS comply with GDPR, CCPA, and applicable local laws.
  • We follow API policies of connected services, including Gmail, Outlook, and others.
  • Security assessments are conducted as required by service providers.

8. Consent Management

  • Every integration request clearly explains what access is needed.
  • A centralized consent management portal lets you view, update, or revoke permissions across all connected services.
  • Preferences are updated in real-time.

9. Risks & User Recommendations

  • Using SalesOS involves linking accounts and granting access.
  • Risks include potential misuse if accounts are shared or compromised.

Best Practice:

Use a dedicated email account for SalesOS and avoid using personal accounts. Enable strong authentication (like 2FA) on all linked accounts.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your data.
  • Port your data to another service.
  • Receive updates on any changes to this Privacy Policy.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be communicated via email and posted on the Platform.
